# Sidekick Sales Playbook

## What is Sidekick?

Sidekick is an AI-powered SMS assistant for frontline operations. Workers text a phone number to report issues, ask questions, request supplies, and complete work orders. Sidekick automatically creates work orders, assigns technicians, answers questions from the company's knowledge base, and works in any language. Managers get a real-time dashboard showing everything happening across their sites, whether that's a plant, shop, yard, warehouse, or service operation.

No app to install. No training required. Workers just text.

---

## The Problem We Solve

Industrial and shop-floor businesses run on tribal knowledge — the stuff your best technician, supervisor, estimator, or operator knows that's never written down. When that person retires, gets sick, or switches shifts, that knowledge disappears.

Meanwhile:
- Workers waste 20-30 minutes per incident hunting for a supervisor or digging through binders
- Managers find out about problems hours later through word of mouth
- Work orders get lost on whiteboards or in email threads
- New hires take months to get up to speed because knowledge lives in people's heads
- 40% of frontline workers speak Spanish as a first language — most systems only work in English

**Sidekick fixes all of this with something every worker already has: a phone that can text.**

## Full Feature Breakdown

### SMS Worker Experience
- **Issue Reporting:** Workers text a description of any problem. Sidekick creates a prioritized work order automatically.
- **Photo Analysis:** Workers send a photo of broken equipment. AI identifies the machine, assesses severity, and attaches the image to the work order.
- **Knowledge Q&A:** Workers text any question — "What PPE do I need in the welding area?" — and get instant answers from the company's knowledge base.
- **Supply Requests:** Workers text when they need parts or materials. Sidekick checks inventory and creates a supply request.
- **Work Order Updates:** Workers text START or COMPLETE to update work order status. Parts used get logged automatically.
- **Safety Checklists:** Workers text CHECKLIST to run a shift safety check via text.
- **Multilingual:** Workers text in any language. Sidekick auto-detects and responds in their language. Dashboard shows everything in English.
- **Worker Onboarding:** Workers text JOIN + a code to connect. No app download, no account setup.

### AI Engine
- **AI Triage:** Every issue gets automatically classified by priority (critical/high/medium/low), category, suspected root cause, and suggested parts.
- **Auto-Assignment:** Sidekick picks the best technician based on skills, current workload, and past repair history.
- **Tribal Knowledge Capture:** Every completed work order automatically generates a knowledge article — problem, fix, parts used — saved permanently.
- **Critical Incident Alerts:** Manager gets an immediate SMS for critical/high-priority issues.
- **Predictive Health Scores:** Asset health scores update automatically based on work order history and maintenance patterns.

### Manager Dashboard
- **Overview:** One-glance view of what needs attention — critical WOs, blocked items, unanswered questions.
- **Inbox:** Action queue for everything that needs a manager decision. Unanswered questions, unassigned jobs, critical issues.
- **Work Orders:** Full list with filters by status, priority, category, assignee. Click into any WO for AI triage details, manager corrections, and status updates.
- **Assets:** Every piece of equipment with health scores, maintenance history, and related knowledge articles.
- **Team:** Full workforce directory — roles, skills, certifications, location assignments. QR code for workers to join.
- **Knowledge Base:** Auto-generated knowledge articles from completed work orders. Manager review and approval workflow. AI-generated reports.
- **Updates:** Push new SOPs, policy changes, or equipment notes via text, voice memo, or file upload.
- **Settings:** Notification preferences, work order categories and priorities, location management.
- **Multi-Location:** Switch between sites in the sidebar. Everything filters — work orders, assets, team, analytics.
- **CSV Export:** Export work orders and assets to CSV, scoped by location.
- **Daily Digests:** Automated SMS and email summaries of daily operations sent to managers.

## How to Pitch Sidekick

### Opening (30 seconds)

"How do your workers report issues on the floor today?"

*Listen. They'll say: radio, walk to the office, call the supervisor, whiteboard, paper form, or "they just figure it out."*

"What if they could just text? One text from the floor — Sidekick creates a work order, classifies the priority, assigns the right technician, and alerts the manager. Five seconds, no training."

### The Three Selling Points

**1. Zero Friction**
"There's no app to download, no login to remember, no training session to schedule. Workers text a phone number. That's it. You can be live in five minutes."

*Why this matters:* Every other CMMS requires weeks of rollout, IT involvement, and training. Adoption is always the bottleneck. With Sidekick, 100% of your workforce can use it on day one because 100% of them already know how to text.

**2. Tribal Knowledge Capture**
"Every time a work order gets completed, Sidekick automatically writes up what happened — the problem, the fix, the parts used — and saves it as a knowledge article. Next time someone has the same problem, any worker can text and get the answer instantly."

*Why this matters:* Industrial businesses lose critical institutional knowledge every time a veteran technician, estimator, or lead leaves. Sidekick captures that knowledge automatically, without anyone filling out forms or writing documentation.

**3. Works in Any Language**
"A worker texts in Spanish, they get help in Spanish. The manager sees everything in English on the dashboard. Sidekick handles the translation automatically."

*Why this matters:* A huge share of frontline industrial workers in the US speak Spanish as a first language. Most operational software is English-only, which means a huge portion of the workforce is left out. Sidekick includes everyone.

## Objection Handling

### "We already have a CMMS."
"Great — how's adoption? Most CMMS tools have 20-30% adoption on the floor because workers don't want to log into another app. Sidekick isn't a replacement for your CMMS — it's the front door. Workers text, Sidekick creates the work order."

### "Our workers won't use it."
"Do your workers text? Then they'll use it. There's literally nothing to learn. We've seen 90%+ adoption within the first week because there's zero behavior change."

### "What about data security?"
"Every company's data is completely isolated. Session-based authentication, encrypted connections, and company-scoped access controls. Workers can only see their own company's information."

### "We're not ready for AI."
"Your workers don't know they're using AI. They're just texting. The AI works behind the scenes. If it gets something wrong, managers correct it in the dashboard, and it learns."

### "It's too expensive."
"How much does one hour of downtime, rework, or delayed jobs cost you? At $299/month for Starter, if Sidekick prevents one meaningful operational miss in an entire month, it's already paid for itself. The knowledge capture alone is worth it when your next senior tech retires."

### "What about non-English speakers?"
"That's one of our strongest features. Sidekick auto-detects the worker's language and responds in kind. The manager dashboard shows everything in English."

### "We need to think about it."
"Totally understand. How about this — try it for a month. If you're not seeing value, we'll refund the entire first month. No questions asked." *(Only use when prospect is on the fence — see Money-Back Guarantee section above.)*

### "Can it integrate with our existing systems?"
"On Enterprise, yes — we have API access and can build custom integrations. On Starter and Pro, Sidekick works standalone and exports to CSV so you can pull data into any system."

### "What if the AI gets it wrong?"
"Managers can correct anything in the dashboard — change priority, reassign, edit the root cause. The AI learns from every correction. And workers always get an acknowledgment, even if the classification isn't perfect."
