Employee Handbook - EDS Manufacturing

Welcome to EDS Manufacturing! This handbook contains important policies and procedures.

BREAK TIMES
Morning break: 10:00 AM - 10:15 AM
Lunch break: 12:00 PM - 12:30 PM
Afternoon break: 3:00 PM - 3:15 PM
All breaks must be taken in the designated break room on the second floor.

PARKING
Employees must park in Lot B located behind the main building.
Visitor parking is in Lot A near the front entrance.
Do not park in fire lanes or handicap spots without proper permits.
Motorcycles should park in the designated motorcycle area in Lot B.

PPE REQUIREMENTS
All floor workers must wear the following Personal Protective Equipment:
- Safety glasses at all times on the production floor
- Steel-toe boots (no sneakers or sandals)
- High visibility vest
- Hard hat in designated areas
- Hearing protection in zones marked with noise warnings
- Gloves when handling chemicals or sharp materials

REPORTING INCIDENTS
If you witness or are involved in a workplace incident:
1. Ensure your immediate safety and the safety of others
2. Notify your supervisor immediately
3. Call 911 if there is a medical emergency
4. Complete an incident report form within 24 hours
5. Submit the form to HR or your supervisor

ATTENDANCE POLICY
Work hours are 7:00 AM to 3:30 PM Monday through Friday.
You must clock in within 5 minutes of your scheduled start time.
Three tardies in a month will result in a written warning.
Call your supervisor at least 2 hours before your shift if you cannot come to work.

VACATION AND TIME OFF
Full-time employees receive 10 days of paid vacation per year.
Vacation requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance.
PTO can be used for sick days, personal days, or vacation.
Unused PTO does not roll over to the next year.

SAFETY PROCEDURES
Always follow lockout/tagout procedures when servicing equipment.
Keep your work area clean and free of hazards.
Report any unsafe conditions to your supervisor immediately.
Fire extinguishers are located at each exit and in the break room.
Emergency exits are marked with green signs - know your evacuation route.
